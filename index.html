Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need for international travel?

+

You'll need a valid passport, visa (if required), and any necessary travel documents. Always check the specific requirements for your destination country.

How far in advance should I book my trip?

+

For international flights, it's recommended to book 3-6 months in advance. Domestic trips can usually be booked 1-3 months ahead.

What's the best time to visit Europe?

+

The best time is typically late spring (May-June) or early fall (September-October) when crowds are smaller and weather is pleasant.
